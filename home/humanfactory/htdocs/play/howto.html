In order to play Human Factory, you will need to download the game and intall it on your computer. Once everything is installed, you're ready to go!

Aim of the Game
The aim of the game is to guide your nanobot through the digestive tract and get as many points as you can. There are three ways to get points:
  1. Take photos of as many different objects in the digestive system as possible. The more interesting or different the object is, the more points you'll score!
  2. Bump into Mr Sweetcorn (if you can catch him!) and read his bizarre facts to get more points.
  3. Answer questions correctly in the quizzes at the end of each section. All the answers are in the info panel given to you when you click on an object.

Nanobot
The nanobot is the vehicle you guide through the convoluted tunnels of the digestive tract. At the beginning of the game it is swallowed along with a slice of pizza. Being equipped with a camera, it enables you to take photos of what you see as the pizza is digested. These photos can then earn you points!

Use the keyboard arrow keys to move your nanobot. If you get stuck in a tight corner, try reversing.



Taking Photos
Click on the small on/off button to toggle between camera view and external view.

Once in camera view, use the keyboard Page Up and Page Down keys to look up and down.

Aim the crosshair at the object you want a photo of and click the camera button to take a photo.

The number tells you how many photos you have left.

Deleting Photos
You can only take a maximum of eight photos in each organ so there may be times when you want to delete a photo and replace it with another one.

Click the scrapbook button to go to the scrapbook. Here you can view all the photos you've taken and delete the ones you don't want. You can also read info about the main object in your photo.

Info
To find out what an object is, roll your mouse over it and the name will appear in the Item box on the bottom left of your screen.

To read information about an object, simply click on it and an info panel will appear.

At the end of the info panel you will be given the choice to either close it or to go to the magnified view of that object.


 
Magnifier
In addition to the normal view inside the body, there is also a magnified view of food molecules and enzymes

Click the magnifier to switch it to the ON position (green).

The cursor will now change to a magnifying glass.

Next, click on the object you want to magnify.

Once in magnified view, you can use the arrow keys and Page Up / Down keys to get a closer look at the molecules.

Just as in normal view, you can also click magnified objects for info and take photos!

You exit the magnified view by clicking the red button in the top right of the screen









Body Map
You can get an overview of the digestive system and your location by clicking on the Body Map button

Your location is shown by the glowing area on the map

You can use the arrow keys and Page Up / Down keys to get a closer look at the body.

Clicking an organ brings up info and also allows you to jump directly there.

You can't take photos when you're looking at the Body Map





Mr Sweetcorn
Mr Sweetcorn is a piece of sweetcorn that was eaten with the pizza. He travels with you through the digestive system (why isn't he digested?) and acts as your guide. If you bump into him, he will tell you interesting facts about the organ you are in. He's a bit of a tease and will do his best to run away from you. If you do manage to catch him however, youll be rewarded with extra points.

At the end of each section, Mr Sweetcorn will test your knowledge in a short quiz. All the answers to the quiz can be found by reading the info for the objects in that section. The more answers you get right, the more points you score!
